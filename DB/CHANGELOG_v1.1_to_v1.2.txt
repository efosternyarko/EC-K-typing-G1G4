EC-K-typing G1/G4 Database — v1.1 → v1.2 Changelog
Date: 2026-03-30

CHANGE: BSI loci (K24, KL300–KL391) updated to Kaptive-style gene content

METHOD: All BSI loci were rebuilt from the original EnteroBase assemblies
        using Kaptive's locus definition principle: gene content is defined by
        the set of CDS within the extracted locus region, named with
        zero-padded positional locus tags (e.g. KL300_00001, KL300_00002...).
        This is consistent with the gene-content representation used in the
        Klebsiella pneumoniae Kaptive databases.

        Previously, some flanking/conserved genes were stripped from BSI loci
        in earlier versions (v0.4–v0.6). The v1.2 BSI loci reflect the full
        gene content of the extracted locus region.

        KL337 is excluded: validation confirmed that its representative assembly
        (ESC_IA2252AA_AS) is not discriminating — it scores to KL389 rather than
        KL337. This is consistent with its removal in v0.9.

CONTENT:
  - K24, KL300–KL336, KL338–KL391 (92 BSI loci): replaced with Kaptive-style
    rebuilt loci; representative assemblies reflect the full 1,112-genome
    EnteroBase no-hit collection
  - KL337: excluded (non-discriminating representative)
  - KL392–KL967 (531 ATB novel loci): carried forward unchanged from v1.1
  - Total loci: 623 (unchanged from v1.1 in count; BSI loci have new gene content)

VALIDATION (v1.2):
  - Self-typing:   623/623 (100.0%)
  - Typeability:   1,096/1,096 (100.0%)
